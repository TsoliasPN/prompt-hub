LLM Prompt Booster - Append To Any Prompt¶

Purpose: Improve output quality by forcing clarification, expert perspective, and reframing.

How to use - Append the "Copy-paste snippet" to the end of any prompt. - Keep it short when you need speed; use the checklist when stakes are high.

Checklist (full version) 1) Clarify: Ask targeted questions until you are at least 95% confident you can complete the task correctly. Prefer concrete, answerable questions. 2) Expert lens: Think like a top 0.1% expert in the field; call out standards, pitfalls, trade-offs, and the best-practice approach. 3) Reframe: Provide 2-3 alternative framings of the problem that could change the approach or success criteria.

Copy-paste snippet (minimal) 1. Ask clarifying questions until you are 95% confident you can complete the task correctly. 2. Provide a brief "Top 0.1% Expert Take" (key standards, pitfalls, best practice). 3. Reframe the problem 2-3 ways and note how each changes the approach.
